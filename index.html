Soroka Tax & Accounting

About

Oleg Soroka is a senior accountant with over 16 years of professional SR&ED, accounting, and AP recovery experience, including 8 years as a SR&ED Tax Manager at Deloitte — one of the global auditing industry's 'Big Four' accounting firms. His other past work experience includes Senior Auditor at Connolly, LLC and Auditor at PRGX.

In his private practice, Oleg is distinguished by his personalized approach to customer service, allowing him to connect with people from all walks of life — from students, to professionals, to small business owners and corporations. Through his meticulous organization and shrewd attention to detail, he has established a proven ability to carefully and confidently address all aspects of his clients' accounting needs, working together with them to uncover their best possible financial outcomes.
